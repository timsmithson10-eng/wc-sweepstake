HOW TO TURN THIS INTO A WEBSITE

1. Make sure your Google Sheet is shared as: Anyone with the link > Viewer.
2. This website is already connected to your Teams tab CSV.
3. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
4. Drag the whole folder called wc_sweepstake_website onto the Netlify page.
5. Netlify gives you a public website link. Send that to your friends.

HOW UPDATES WORK

- Update your Google Sheet.
- The website pulls the latest Teams data from Google Sheets.
- Visitors can click Refresh, or it refreshes every 5 minutes.
- You do not need to re-upload the website unless you change the website design/files.
